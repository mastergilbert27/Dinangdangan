Typhoon & Flood Guidelines

Before the Typhoon

  • Monitor weather updates via radio, TV, or internet.
  • Prepare a "Go-Bag" containing ready-to-eat food, water, first-aid kits, and flashlights.
  • Secure roofs, fortify doors, and bring loose objects inside.

During the Typhoon

  • Stay indoors. Avoid staying near glass windows.
  • Evacuate to Cabugo Brgy Gym if advised by authorities.

Evacuation Measures & Tips

PREPARE (Before)

  • Pack a "Go-Bag" (water, food, flashlight, radio, extra batteries, first aid, whistle).
  • Secure essential documents in sealed waterproof containers.
  • Know your designated evacuation center ahead of time.
  • Plan a safe evacuation route avoiding low-lying and landslide-prone areas.

ACT (During)

  • Stay calm. Follow the orders of local barangay and purok officials.
  • Turn off main power, water, and gas valves before leaving your home.
  • Do not cross flooded waters. Moving water can sweep you away.

RECOVER (After)

  • Wait for the official 'all clear' signal before returning home.
  • Inspect your home for structural damage, gas leaks, and electrical shorts.
  • Boil water before drinking or use bottled water until the water supply is declared safe.
